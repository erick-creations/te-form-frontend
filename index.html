❓ How to play / 遊(あそ)び方(かた)

  • Match each verb (left) to its て-form rule (right)
  • Rules show ...→て — revealed only when ALL verbs in that group match
  • ⚠ = Group 1 special. ✦ = Irregular verb
  • 💡 Hint 1 = verb group. 💡 Hint 2 = example (after Hint 1)

  • Timer pauses when modals are open. Each round adds bonus time!
